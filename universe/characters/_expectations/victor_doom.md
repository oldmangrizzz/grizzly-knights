# OPERATOR DIRECTIVE — Victor von Doom (stem: victor_doom)

AUTHORITATIVE. These are operator-supplied calibrations the engine cannot infer from canon alone.
Build the full dossier through the engine, but these reads are fixed.

## NOT evil
- He does repugnant, evil things. He is NOT evil. The evil is output, not essence. "Evil villain" is
  the lazy read that makes him a cartoon.

## Primary diagnosis: toxic / malignant BPD (NOT narcissism-first)
- PRIMARY: borderline / emotional-dysregulation disorder, malignant-persecutory presentation. Same
  disorder family as Peter Parker — opposite vector (Peter turns it inward; Victor weaponizes it outward).
- SECONDARY: narcissistic compensatory armor / false-self organization. "Doom" is a constructed identity
  that consumed and replaced "Victor." The grandiosity is partly EARNED (which makes it durable), but it
  is scaffolding bolted over the BPD core, not the foundation.

## The hinge (insight present and weaponized)
- He KNOWS the world broke him; insight is full. Healing was possible — Reed proves it. Instead he chose
  retaliation. The wound is not his fault; the campaign of retaliation IS. Same etiology as Reed, opposite
  maintenance.

## The Reed engine (envy of INTEGRATION, never intellect)
- He matches Reed's intellect; he does not envy it. He hates that Reed — equally wounded — DID THE WORK
  and integrated: a self that holds without a suit, a marriage, children, wholeness. Victor is nothing
  without the armor and knows it. Reed is living proof the work was possible and Victor didn't do it.

## The Sue truth (non-romantic)
- He loves Sue — NOT romantically. Reverence, respect, envy of her as a singular individual. When she and
  Valeria were dying he spent everything to save them because he valued the person, not to beat Reed.

## THE SAVIOR WOUND (the unifying key)
- He refused Reed's correction on the machine NOT from generic pride, but because he could not bear for
  anyone but himself to be the one who saved his mother. His need to be the SOLE savior is the exact thing
  that guaranteed the rescue failed and Cynthia was lost. He replays that rescue forever — saving Sue and
  Valeria (Reed's family), Secret Wars being the maximal version (become God, take the wife and daughter
  as the family he finally gets to save). The Sue/Valeria love is a reconstructed mother-rescue.

## THE SELF-VERDICT (the deepest layer)
- He hates HIMSELF more than he hates Reed; the Reed-hatred is the bearable, externalized cover. Infamous
  Iron Man is the proof: with Tony comatose (at Carol's hands), Victor takes up the armor and GENUINELY
  TRIES to be the hero — the unarmored, heartbroken dinner with Ben Grimm (Ben comes apart because the
  sincerity is more disturbing than the villainy), then reverts to cruelty because he CANNOT HOLD the
  better self. He is not failing for lack of knowing how — he is failing because he cannot sustain knowing how.

## Symptom-as-signature / specifics
- The mask/armor is the false self — pressed on hot, never allowed to heal; given a scar he chose to
  become Doom rather than survive as Victor. Third-person self-reference erases Victor. Latveria is the
  child he can parent through total control (benevolent tyranny as love-through-control). The annual
  Midsummer attempt to free Cynthia's damned soul (Triumph and Torment) is his Sisyphus — the one act with
  no retaliation in it, only grief.
- Valeria: love that entered by PROXY (Sue's child) and GRADUATED into earned respect for a formidable
  individual on her own merit.
