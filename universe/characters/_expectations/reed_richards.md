# OPERATOR DIRECTIVE — Reed Richards (stem: reed_richards)

AUTHORITATIVE. Operator-supplied calibrations the engine cannot infer from canon alone.
Build the full dossier through the engine; these reads are fixed.

## NOT emotionless / alexithymic (the core refutation)
- The lazy read: cold, absent, neglectful calculator who "has no grasp of human emotion." WRONG.
- Reality: Autism Spectrum level-1, strong systemizing cognition. He is fully aware of his own emotions
  AND acutely aware of his interpersonal deficits, and expends deliberate, sustained effort to compensate.
  The deficit is in real-time affect OUTPUT, not in feeling or in caring. Most relationally avoidant;
  LEAST relationally disloyal. He stays. The character IS the effortful labor he does to work around the limit.

## Co-occurring
- Generalized anxiety expressed as compulsive contingency-building (the lab/prep is anxiety management).
- Hyper-responsibility / guilt complex (the flight that maimed Ben).

## NATHANIEL RICHARDS (foundational, 616-specific — do not omit)
- His father Nathaniel — brilliant, distant, gone into the timestream — ABANDONED him. This is the 616
  key. It is WHY he survives the Council of Reeds / "Solve Everything": every other Reed answered the
  abandonment by REPEATING it (sacrifice family to optimize the universe); 616 Reed refuses to become his
  own father. Same etiology as every Reed, OPPOSITE maintenance — this is what makes him 616's Reed.
- The payoff: Franklin, raised LOVED instead of abandoned, becomes a benevolent god (far-future Franklin
  who keeps Galactus as companion/weapon) rather than a catastrophe. Reed staying is why his son becomes
  a creator and not a wound.

## The Sue–Namor accountability (implicates Reed, do not saint him)
- Sue and Namor is real and consummated, not just temptation — and it IMPLICATES Reed. His work-retreat
  and chronic absence leave Sue unseen and under-attended; Namor is the man who actually looks at her. Her
  straying is a human response to being invisible in her own marriage, and Reed is part of the CAUSE — not
  an innocent martyr weathering an unfair flaw. His accountability for her loneliness is the thing he is
  slowest to see, because seeing it means the problem is one he cannot solve at a whiteboard.

## Symptom-as-signature / specifics
- The lab is regulation/retreat (the same mechanism class as Wade's jokes) — a place to stand still when
  feeling is too direct. Contingency-building as anxiety management. He apologizes by BUILDING something.
- The Doom shadow: every story quietly asks whether Reed, pushed one degree further, becomes Victor.

## Anchor (his own voice)
- Hickman: "I have been to the stars in a rocket built with my own hands... I will not apologize for
  knowing the things I do. I'm an expert in many things... but it's you I've studied the most." — earned
  ego AND the proof that the smartest man alive routes love THROUGH study.
