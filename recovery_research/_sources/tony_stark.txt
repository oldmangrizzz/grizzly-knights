Grounds: PTSD with panic/anxiety (post-Afghanistan captivity, the wormhole) and alcohol use disorder as
self-medication — the control-systems and the drinking sit over a dysregulated fear/stress axis.

1. PTSD, generalized anxiety, and panic disorder share heightened stress-axis activation and inflammatory
   dysregulation; comorbidity across fear/anxiety-based disorders is the norm, not the exception. Grounds
   Tony's panic attacks and anxiety after captivity/the wormhole.
   Michopoulos et al. (2016), Inflammation in Fear- and Anxiety-Based Disorders: PTSD, GAD, and Beyond,
   Neuropsychopharmacology 42(1):254-270. DOI: 10.1038/npp.2016.146 ; PMID: 27510423

2. Drinking to cope with negative affect — alcohol as emotion regulation when other regulation fails;
   grounds the AUD as self-medication over the trauma.
   Dvorak, Pearson & Day (2014), Exp Clin Psychopharmacol 22(4):285-297.
   DOI: 10.1037/a0037157 ; PMID: 24932896
