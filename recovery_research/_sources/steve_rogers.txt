CLINICAL SOURCES — Steve Rogers / Captain America (retrieved from PubMed; cite in clinical_provenance).
Grounds: survivor guilt and complicated/displacement grief — he outlived his entire era (Bucky, Peggy,
the whole generation) and woke into a world where everyone he loved was gone or aged; grief that is
chronic, under-processed, and buried in duty.

1. Grief aetiology and course: loss produces shock, disbelief, anger, and distress; "complicated grief"
   is a superimposed process that prolongs and worsens mourning, intensified by sudden/traumatic loss and
   by avoidance. Grounds Steve's chronic, displaced, duty-buried grief and survivor guilt.
