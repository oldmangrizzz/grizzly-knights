# UATU ANALYTICAL WORKUP: BRUNO CARRELLI

**Classification:** Pre-profile intelligence file. Subject is a civilian-tier supporting figure in the Kamala Khan / Ms. Marvel orbit (Marvel Comics, 2014–present). All inferences derived from published behavioral record.

---

## 1. HISTORY

Bruno Carrelli is introduced in *Ms. Marvel* (2014) #1 (Wilson/Alphona) as a Jersey City teenager working the counter at the Circle Q convenience store, already established as Kamala Khan's oldest and closest friend. The historical baseline is critical: Bruno is a working-class Italian-American kid in a predominantly Pakistani-American Muslim social milieu, and his fluency within that milieu — knowing Kamala's family rules, deferring to her father, understanding the cultural weight of her decisions — signals years of immersive proximity prior to story-start. He is, from issue one, the person who knows her best and the person whose feelings she most consistently fails to read.

**Formative situation (pre-issue 1, inferable):** Bruno is the second of two brothers, with an older brother, Vick, who has criminal entanglements (Inventor arc, #6–11). His parents are largely absent from on-panel life; he supports himself through Circle Q wages and is functionally a self-supporting minor by mid-series. He is academically gifted — sufficient to be recruited to a Bard early-college program in Wakanda (#13, 2016 relaunch, the "Last Days" / post-Secret Wars run). His intellectual identity is established early: he engineers Kamala's costume polymer, builds her bio-kinetic energy meter, repeatedly invents ad hoc tech to support her vigilantism.

**Arc 1 — The Inventor cycle (2014 vol., #6–11):** Vick's debt to the Inventor pulls Bruno into direct conflict with a supervillain. Bruno's response pattern crystallizes here: he triages family loyalty (covering for Vick) against Kamala's safety, and consistently chooses to put himself bodily in harm's way rather than ask Kamala to risk hers. He suffers no powers; he chooses the risk anyway. This is the establishing instance of a lifelong pattern: *Bruno absorbs cost so Kamala doesn't have to.*

**Arc 2 — The Mike Miller relationship (#12 onward, 2015):** After Kamala fails to register his romantic interest — and after she has explicitly told him she cannot date him for religious/familial reasons — Bruno begins dating Michaela "Mike" Miller. The relationship is genuine, not rebound theater; he is good to Mike and she to him. But the triangulated dynamic with Kamala persists: Mike herself eventually names it (Civil War II tie-in, #12 of 2016 vol.) and ends the relationship because Bruno cannot fully decenter Kamala.

**Arc 3 — Civil War II / The Hand Injury (2016 vol., #12–13):** The hinge event of Bruno's life. During the predictive-policing crisis, Bruno attempts to stop Kamala from a course of action he believes will destroy her; in the resulting conflict (involving Becky St. Jude / Discord and a lab explosion engineered around Kamala's predictive-justice campaign), Bruno's hand is catastrophically maimed. He sustains permanent nerve damage and partial loss of fine motor control. Compounding the trauma: Kamala's relationship with him fractures, he loses his academic standing, and he departs for the Wakanda program in self-imposed exile (#13–14).

**Arc 4 — Wakanda exile and return (2016 vol., #14 through 2017 arcs):** Off-page, Bruno spends roughly a year in Wakanda. He returns altered: quieter, more guarded, with a new girlfriend (Kaylee), and with the chronic injury normalized into his daily life. The return arc (#19 onward) shows him deliberately maintaining distance from Kamala, then gradually re-integrating but never fully restoring the prior intimacy. Kaylee eventually exits the picture.

**Arc 5 — Late-vol. and Magnificent Ms. Marvel era (2019, Saladin Ahmed run):** Bruno is reintegrated as core support cast; the romantic tension is reactivated but handled with more maturity by both parties. By the time of Kamala's death-and-resurrection cycle (*The Death of Ms. Marvel*, 2023; *Fallen Friend*) and her mutant retcon, Bruno is again positioned as primary confidant.

**Pattern of evolution:** Early Bruno is openly pining, openly self-sacrificing, openly the brain-of-the-operation with little ego cost. Post-injury Bruno carries a depressive register, more interiorized resentment, more careful boundary-management. The arc is, in the truest sense, a coming-of-age via *narcissistic injury* in the clinical sense — the loss of the idealized self (the brilliant kid going to Caltech/Wakanda; the indispensable friend; the body that worked).

## 2. MEDICAL

**Baseline:** Adolescent male, Italian-American, normative development. No congenital conditions in record. No supernatural augmentation; entirely unpowered in a powered cohort — a fact with substantial psychological consequence (see §3).

**Acute injury — right hand (the Inventor/Discord lab explosion sequelae, #12–13, 2016 vol.):** Severe trauma to the dominant hand. Visible on-panel sequelae include scarring, contracture, and chronic functional impairment. He is shown unable to perform fine soldering work at prior speed; tool use is awkward; he develops compensatory left-handed technique over the Wakanda year. The injury is **permanent**. In real-world terms this presentation is consistent with crush/blast injury with peripheral nerve involvement — likely partial median/ulnar damage — producing chronic neuropathic pain and reduced grip/precision. He is never shown on analgesics on-panel, but the behavioral record (intermittent wincing, flexing, reflexive cradling of the hand under stress) is consistent with ongoing low-grade chronic pain.

**Psychological sequelae of the injury:** This is the medically central fact. For an identity organized around *manual* intelligence — building, soldering, fabricating — partial loss of the building hand is an attack on the self-concept, not merely the body. The post-injury withdrawal, the geographic flight to Wakanda, the reorganization of his relationship to Kamala — all are downstream of a somatic event that he experiences as having been *caused by* (or at minimum *in service of*) the superhero life he never asked for. This is the substrate of a trauma response (see §4).

**Substance use:** None depicted. Bruno is notably abstemious in a YA-comics frame; no alcohol, no other substances.

**Other:** Standard adolescent injuries from being a non-powered participant in superhero scrapes (contusions, one or two on-panel knockings-unconscious in early issues). No chronic illness. Sleep is implicitly poor in late 2014 vol. and post-injury (background panels show him at the workbench at night).

## 3. PSYCHOLOGICAL

**Attachment style — anxious-preoccupied with strong caregiver overlay.** Bruno's relational template, as inferable from behavior, is to secure connection through *being needed*. He volunteers competence (the tech, the cover-stories, the rides) before he is asked. He tolerates Kamala's repeated obliviousness to his romantic interest with a forbearance that, in a securely attached subject, would have ruptured the friendship; he metabolizes it instead. The function of compulsive usefulness is to make abandonment economically irrational for the other party. Etiology: parental absence and elder-brother delinquency (Vick) suggest a family system in which Bruno's role was the *parentified competent one* — the kid who keeps the household, and later the friend-group, functional. Parentified children classically present exactly this profile: high agency, low entitlement, chronic underestimation of own needs, displaced anger.

**Defenses (mature-to-neurotic band):**
- *Sublimation* — primary and adaptive. Affect → tech projects. When Kamala is in danger or unavailable, Bruno builds. The polymer costume, the bio-kinetic meter, the Wakanda research — all are downstream of unbearable feeling channeled into instrumented mastery.
- *Intellectualization* — secondary. He metabolizes the Inventor crisis, the Civil War II rupture, and the hand injury through reasoned analysis rather than affective expression. He is verbally articulate about *Kamala's* feelings (he reads her superbly) and consistently terse about his own.
- *Reaction formation* — present but not dominant. When wounded by Kamala, his first move is increased helpfulness, not withdrawal. The Wakanda exile is the moment this defense finally collapses and he permits himself a withdrawing response.
- *Displacement* — when angry at Kamala, he is more often visibly short with Vick, Mike, or Kaylee. The actual target is protected.

**Affect regulation:** High threshold for affect display, low threshold for affect *experience*. He feels things intensely and shows them sparingly. Under sufficient pressure, regulation fails into either flat depressive withdrawal (post-injury arc) or one sharp confrontational outburst followed by withdrawal (Civil War II argument with Kamala). He does not rage; he does not weep on-panel readily; he goes quiet, leaves, and builds something.

**Cognitive style:** Convergent technical reasoning with strong systems-thinking; can model second-order consequences (this is what makes him correctly predict the Civil War II catastrophe before Kamala does). Notably, his cognitive accuracy *about others* is high while his metacognition *about himself* is poor — a classic split in parentified, caregiving subjects.

**Self-concept:** Organized around competence and reliability. The implicit equation is *I am worth keeping because I am useful*. This is fragile. The hand injury is psychologically devastating not only because of pain or impairment but because it threatens the load-bearing wall of the self-concept. Post-injury, the self-concept does not so much *heal* as *narrow* — he becomes a quieter, less reaching, more guarded version of himself who has accepted a smaller life. This is convalescent, not flourishing.

**Compensatory mechanisms — positive:** Tech mastery; deep loyalty as a stable identity; the moral satisfaction of being the one who *does the right thing quietly*. He genuinely enjoys building, and the enjoyment is not entirely defensive.

**Compensatory mechanisms — negative:** Chronic self-erasure; a tendency to construct his life around someone else's gravity well; difficulty receiving care (he is awkward when Kamala tries to apologize, when Mike tries to know him); the implicit belief that his own desires are an imposition.

**Interpersonal valence around the powered/unpowered axis:** Bruno operates daily among people whose bodies do extraordinary things. He has chosen, over and over, *not* to seek augmentation. This is meaningful. Where a more grandiose subject would have made himself a tech-armored vigilante, Bruno builds for Kamala and remains the guy at the counter. The refusal to compensate via power is itself a form of psychological maturity — and also, less flatteringly, a form of staying small.

## 4. PSYCHIATRIC

**Working clinical impression** (derived strictly from observed behavior):

**Primary — Adjustment Disorder with Depressed Mood, chronic, post-traumatic features (post-#12–13).** Following the hand injury and the rupture with Kamala, Bruno presents with: social withdrawal, geographic flight, anhedonia (Wakanda-era panels show him performing rather than enjoying his work), reduced affect range, and a new pessimistic explanatory style. These persist > 6 months, warranting consideration of **Persistent Depressive Disorder (dysthymia)** as a more accurate frame for the post-return Bruno. The depressive register never fully clears; it is *baseline*.

**Trauma features without full PTSD:** The injury produces avoidance (he avoids the lab, then avoids Kamala, then avoids Jersey City entirely) and likely intrusive cognition about the event (inferable from his reluctance to discuss it on return), but the record does not show the hyperarousal cluster, and functioning recovers sufficiently that full PTSD is **not** the right call. *Acute Stress Disorder* would have been appropriate at the time of injury; what persists is sub-threshold post-traumatic adjustment.

**Trait-level: prominent dependent and obsessive-compulsive personality *features*, sub-clinical.** He shows the dependent-style pattern of organizing identity around one attachment figure, difficulty initiating projects independent of that figure's needs, and difficulty expressing disagreement for fear of loss of approval. He shows the OCPD-band pattern of perfectionism in technical work, over-conscientiousness, and rigidity about right-doing. Neither rises to disorder; both shape the picture.

**Differential — what Bruno is NOT, and why:**
- *Not Major Depressive Disorder.* Episodes do not reach severity/duration criteria; functioning is preserved; he continues to work, study, and relate. The depressive coloration is dysthymic, not melancholic.
- *Not PTSD.* See above — avoidance and reexperiencing present; hyperarousal/dissociation absent.
- *Not Avoidant Personality.* His withdrawal is event-driven (post-injury) and selective (he re-engages); the trait-level *baseline* is approach-oriented, not avoidant. He sustains close friendships, romantic relationships, and intellectual community.
- *Not Narcissistic.* The opposite — pathological *low* entitlement, chronic underestimation of own importance. The fragility of the self-concept around competence has *narcissistic structure* (in Kohut's sense — vulnerable to narcissistic injury) without narcissistic *content* (no grandiosity, no entitlement, no exploitativeness).
- *Not codependent in the clinical caretaker-of-addict sense*, though the pattern rhymes: his caretaking is of an *idealized* other (Kamala-the-hero), not a regressing one.

**Etiological summary:** A constitutionally bright, conscientious child in a family system that required him to be the adult; an adolescence organized around one intense friendship that he could not fully claim; a traumatic injury sustained in service of that friendship; and a quiet, partial, ongoing recovery.

## 5. PERSONALITY

**Core drives, ranked:**
1. To be *indispensable* to people he loves — the load-bearing form of his self-worth.
2. To *build* — to convert understanding into working objects; the only consistently autonomous source of pleasure.
3. To *do right* — a strong, quiet moral architecture, more deontological than calculated.
4. Far behind: to be *seen* as himself, not as utility. This drive exists but is chronically suppressed, and its suppression is the engine of most of his suffering.

**Worldview / operational code:** The world is improvable through careful work by careful people; most harm comes from impulsive action by people who haven't thought it through (this is the core of his Civil War II position against Kamala). Loyalty is a duty, not a feeling. Love is shown by labor. Speech is cheap and frequently misleading; the test of caring is what you build, fix, or absorb for someone. He distrusts power — institutional power, superhuman power, the power of charisma — and trusts competence, evidence, and follow-through.

**Interpersonal pattern:** Approaches via service. Tolerates extraordinary asymmetry in being-known versus knowing-the-other. Slow to anger and quick to forgive — *but* the forgiveness is partial; trust, once cracked, is rebuilt only structurally, not fully restored. Romantically, gravitates toward partners who can see him clearly (Mike's clear-sightedness is precisely what makes the relationship end, because what she sees is that he is not fully available). Friendships are few and deep; he is not a roomful-of-acquaintances person.

**Stress response / escalation curve:** Baseline → increased usefulness → terse withdrawal → one sharp confrontation (rare; high-signal when it occurs) → flat depressive withdrawal → flight (geographic or relational) → slow guarded return. He does *not* escalate to aggression, intoxication, or self-harm. The danger sign is the *flatness*, not any acting-out.

**Pressure points:**
- **The hand.** Any reminder of the injury — instrumental, relational, or as evidence of his limits — destabilizes him. He will not show it, but the cost is paid in withdrawal hours later.
- **Being treated as the sidekick / the support staff.** He has internalized the role but resents it; explicit external naming of it wounds disproportionately.
- **Kamala in danger he cannot prevent.** The original wound of his life — that her powers put her in places his competence can't reach — reactivates whenever she is hurt.
- **Vick.** Family loyalty he cannot fully honor or fully sever; a source of ambient guilt.
- **Being thanked.** Genuine gratitude from Kamala destabilizes him more than her obliviousness does, because it threatens to make him feel the full size of his own unanswered want.

**Synthesis:** Bruno Carrelli is the rare comics-cast member whose entire psychological architecture is *civilian* — built from parentification, sublimation, and chronic injury rather than from origin-story trauma or power-induced identity crisis. He is a high-functioning, ethically serious, emotionally constrained young man whose central pathology is that he has organized a whole self around a love he cannot name and a usefulness he cannot put down. The hand injury is the somatic literalization of a psychic condition that preceded it: *I will give the part of me that builds, so that she can be what she is.* He survives this; he is not destroyed by it; but he is durably marked, and the analytical profile must hold both facts simultaneously — the resilience and the cost.
