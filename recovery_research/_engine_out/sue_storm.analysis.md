# UATU ANALYTICAL WORKUP: SUSAN STORM RICHARDS

## Subject Designation: "Invisible Woman" (formerly "Invisible Girl")
## File Classification: Longitudinal Behavioral Reconstruction, 1961–Present

---

## 1. HISTORY

### Pre-Origin Formative Record

Subject is the elder child of Dr. Franklin Storm (surgeon) and Mary Storm. The single most determinative event of her childhood was the death of her mother in an automobile accident when Susan was a young girl — her father, driving, survived but was unable to save his wife in the operating theater afterward. This catastrophic compounding event (loss + paternal disintegration) initiated the central pattern of subject's life: **the elder female forced into emotional caretaker for a man too unmoored to function**. Franklin Storm collapsed into alcoholism, gambling, manslaughter conviction, and abandonment of his children. Susan, still a child, became de facto parent to her younger brother Johnny — a role she has never psychologically vacated, regardless of his age or capacity (see Fantastic Four Annual #1; FF #32; multiple flashback sequences across Lee/Kirby, Byrne, Hickman, Slott runs).

Subject met Reed Richards as a boarder in her aunt's house when she was approximately twelve and he was an adult graduate student. She formed a fixed, idealizing attachment to a man more than a decade her senior who treated her with intellectual distance. **This is the template**: her primary love object is from origin a figure of unattainable cognitive remove whose attention must be earned and is rarely freely given. She pursued modeling and acting in New York partly to remain in his orbit. She agreed to the spaceflight that produced the team's powers explicitly because Reed was going (Fantastic Four #1, 1961).

### Lee/Kirby Era (1961–1970): The Suppressed Phase

Initial characterization is **systematically diminished** — codenamed "Invisible Girl," powers limited to invisibility only, repeatedly written as needing rescue, fainting, distracted by domestic matters. Behaviorally significant: she absorbs this without overt protest for years. She accepts Reed's casual condescension, Ben's protective patronage, Johnny's dismissiveness. Two anomalies puncture the pattern: (a) the Namor arc, where she demonstrably entertains the Sub-Mariner's attentions and weaponizes Reed's jealousy as the only available lever to extract emotional engagement from him (FF #4, #6, #9, #27, #33); (b) the Frightful Four / Madame Medusa arcs where she begins manifesting force-field projection — a power expansion that **coincides chronologically with marriage, pregnancy, and the first stirrings of resentment**.

Marriage to Reed (FF Annual #3, 1965) and the birth of Franklin (Annual #6, 1968) lock her into the structural role she will spend the next fifty years renegotiating.

### Early Bronze Age / Thomas / Conway / Wein: Strain Emerges

The Franklin-as-cosmic-threat arcs (Annihilus, Agatha Harkness fostering) are decisive. Reed shuts down Franklin's mind (FF #141, 1973). Susan **leaves Reed and the team** — first overt rupture. She returns, but the wound is permanent and from this point forward her compliance is conditional. Brief separation arcs recur. She is briefly written as romantically tempted by Namor again during this stretch — pattern: when Reed treats her as object/asset rather than partner, she seeks (or threatens to seek) the man who treats her as sovereign.

### Byrne Era (1981–1986): The Transformation

This is the **single most psychologically formative published period**. John Byrne deliberately rehabilitates the character: renames her Invisible Woman (FF #284); dramatically expands force-field application (offensive constructs, internal organ pressure, planetary-scale shields); writes her as the team's actual tactical anchor. Critically, the **Malice arc** (FF #280–286) makes the subterranean rage text rather than subtext: the Hate-Monger weaponizes her suppressed anger into a dominatrix alter-ego, "Malice, Mistress of Hate," who attacks her family. Subject defeats Malice by acknowledging the rage as her own and integrating it. Reed deliberately provokes her to that breaking point — note: her healthiest single moment of self-actualization is engineered by the husband who caused much of what she's repressing. The miscarriage of her second child during this run (FF #267) is the somatic correlate.

### Post-Byrne to Heroes Reborn

She holds the Byrne-era authority. Daughter Valeria's complicated birth/non-birth/eventual delivery (the Claremont and later Waid runs) compounds her reproductive trauma — by the time Valeria is safely born, subject has lost a pregnancy, had a child weaponized cosmically, and had a second pregnancy effectively rewritten out of existence and back in. **No civilian woman would survive these reproductive events with intact affect; subject does, by displacement onto competence.**

### Waid/Wieringo, Straczynski, Millar, Hickman: Maturation

Increasingly written as the team's **moral and emotional executive**, with Reed deferring to her judgment on ethics. Civil War (2006–2007): she initially follows Reed onto the pro-registration side, then defects to Captain America's side when she cannot stomach Reed's collaboration with Thunderbolts/Negative Zone prison. **This is the defining adult-Susan act**: she leaves her husband over a values disagreement, conducts a guerrilla resistance, and returns only when he capitulates. Hickman's run (2009–2012) elevates her further — diplomat to Atlantis, architect of the Future Foundation's pedagogy, the adult in rooms full of geniuses. Slott's run (2018–) and recent material continue this register.

### Pattern of Evolution

From **silent absorber** → **resentful compliant** → **rupture-and-return cycle** → **integrated authority who can leave**. The arc is genuine growth, but the underlying wound (the parentified child who fears her family will not survive her departure) never fully resolves; it is managed.

## 2. MEDICAL

### Genetic / Constitutional
Cosmic ray exposure (1961 spaceflight) altered cellular structure to permit (a) selective light-bending invisibility of self and other matter, (b) generation of psionic force constructs of effectively unlimited scaling. Power demonstrably **autonomic-linked**: invisibility lapses under unconsciousness; force-field strength scales with emotional state, particularly anger and protective threat-response. This is medically relevant — **her endocrine/limbic activity is now a physical weapon and a physical liability**. Stress is no longer abstract; stress is ordnance.

### Obstetric/Gynecological History
- Pregnancy #1: Franklin (1968), complicated by cosmic-ray-altered physiology requiring Annihilus's Cosmic Control Rod for safe delivery (Annual #6).
- Pregnancy #2: Miscarriage at term, also due to radiation incompatibility (FF #267, 1984). Reed unable to save the child despite heroic intervention. **Significant trauma; never adequately mourned in published record — defended against by return to function.**
- Pregnancy #3: Valeria — initial fetal loss, later restored/reborn via Franklin's reality-altering powers and Doom's intervention. Reproductive history is, in any clinical sense, **catastrophic**.

### Injuries / Bodily Events
Multiple near-deaths; brief death (Hickman run); possession events (Malice, Psycho-Man's emotion-engine, Hate-Monger). Repeated mind-control exposure. Significant cumulative TBI risk from cosmic-tier combat is unmeasurable but presumed.

### Substance Use
Negative for subject. Notable inverse: **paternal alcoholism** is the formative substance-use exposure. Subject's adult abstemiousness and her vigilance around Johnny's recklessness are consistent with adult-child-of-alcoholic compensatory patterns.

### Somatic-Psychological Coupling
Her invisibility is a **somatic metaphor that became literal**. The girl who was overlooked received, from the universe, the power to be overlooked at will. Her force fields — born later, scaled with her assertion — are the somatic metaphor of the boundary she was never permitted as a child. Her body keeps the score legibly.

## 3. PSYCHOLOGICAL

### Attachment
**Anxious-preoccupied with strong earned-secure overlay.** Origin attachment to father (Franklin Storm) was disrupted by traumatic loss of mother and paternal disintegration. Primary adult attachment (Reed) reproduces the structure: a brilliant, emotionally withholding man whose attention must be earned through usefulness. Compensatory caregiving toward Johnny is **classic parentification residue**. Over time — particularly post-Byrne — subject has built an earned-secure overlay: she can now name needs, withdraw consent, leave the field and return on her terms. The underlying anxious template still activates under acute relational threat (Reed disappearances, Johnny endangerment).

### Defenses
Mature defenses dominant: **sublimation** (rage → force-field combat capacity), **altruism** (caretaking as identity), **suppression** (conscious deferral of affect to handle the crisis), **humor** (dry, occasionally cutting). Neurotic defenses present: **reaction formation** (cheerful competence over considerable anger), **intellectualization** (adopted from Reed; she now talks the language of the men around her to be heard). The **Malice episode is the diagnostic moment**: long-term repression produced a dissociative split that required external precipitant to integrate. Post-integration, repression has loosened — she now expresses anger directly. This is real therapeutic progress occurring in the absence of therapy.

### Affect Regulation
High baseline regulation; appears calm under conditions that destabilize peers. Regulation is **effortful, not constitutional** — purchased with chronic suppression. Pressure points: threats to children, condescension that echoes early-marriage Reed, being made invisible *socially* (not powered, but ignored). When these hit, regulation fails fast and force expression follows.

### Cognitive Style
Practical-integrative. Not a scientist's mind by Reed's standard, but a superior **systems-and-people** mind — she reads room dynamics, predicts political consequences, models second-order effects on relationships. Her judgment on ethical questions has, by the modern era, become the team's de facto court of last resort. Pattern: she **arrives at correct answers ahead of Reed on human questions** and is now confident enough to say so.

### Self-Concept
Bifurcated. The conscious self-concept is mother/wife/teammate — relational. Underneath is an unintegrated self that knows she is **the most powerful member of the Fantastic Four** and has known it since the Byrne era. The gap between objective capability and subjective self-presentation has narrowed but not closed. She still defers in framing while overruling in substance.

### Compensatory Mechanisms
- **Positive**: Caregiving channeled into Future Foundation pedagogy — generativity in the Eriksonian sense.
- **Positive**: Diplomatic vocation (Atlantean ambassador) — uses parentified pattern at planetary scale, healthily.
- **Negative**: Will repeatedly forgive Reed for failures that warrant harder consequences; tolerates Johnny's regressions past the point of his benefit.
- **Negative**: Identifies threats to family faster than threats to self; somatically vulnerable to chronic stress.

## 4. PSYCHIATRIC

### Diagnostic Considerations

**Persistent Complex Bereavement / Unresolved Grief (mother, infant son).** Evidence: never narratively given mourning space; resumes function within issues; somatic-symbolic enactments (invisibility, force-shielding family) substitute for processed grief. Sub-clinical but durable.

**PTSD, Chronic, with Dissociative Features (partial / historical).** Criteria support: repeated life-threatening events; reproductive trauma; possession episodes; intrusive re-engagement (Malice resurfaces in modified form across decades); hyperarousal specifically around child-endangerment. The Malice possession is best read clinically as a **trauma-induced dissociative phenomenon exploited by an external agent** — not a primary dissociative disorder, but the substrate was present and is now largely integrated. Does **not** currently meet active criteria; in functional remission.

**Adult Children of Alcoholics syndrome (Woititz framework).** Strong evidence: hyperresponsibility, difficulty asking for help, over-functioning in relationships with under-functioning men, conflict-avoidance trained early then renegotiated late, vigilance toward substance-using or impulsive intimates (Johnny). This is not a DSM diagnosis but it is the most explanatorily powerful single frame.

**Differential — what subject is NOT:**
- **Not Dependent Personality Disorder.** Despite the early-era surface appearance, she demonstrates autonomous decision-making, leaves relationships when warranted (Civil War, Franklin's lobotomy), and has a stable independent self-concept. The dependency is **role-bound and historical**, not structural.
- **Not Histrionic.** Affect display is in fact suppressed, not amplified.
- **Not Codependent in the clinical sense.** She does enable, but she also confronts and exits when limits are reached. Earned-secure trajectory disqualifies pure codependence.
- **Not Major Depressive Disorder.** Dysthymic substrate possible historically; not currently supported.
- **Not Dissociative Identity Disorder.** Malice was external/iatrogenic, not endogenous alter formation; no evidence of switching outside that arc.

### Etiological Summary
Early traumatic loss + parental incapacitation + parentification + adolescent idealization of an emotionally unavailable adult + reproductive catastrophe + chronic high-lethality occupational exposure = a presentation that **should** be far more impaired than it is. The fact that she functions at her observed level is itself the clinical anomaly and is attributable to (a) genuine constitutional resilience, (b) the holding environment of the team-family, and (c) the somatic discharge route her powers provide.

## 5. PERSONALITY

### Core Drives
1. **Keep the family intact.** Above heroism, above marriage, above self. This is pre-rational, set before age ten.
2. **Be seen on her own terms.** A drive that grew slowly from buried to operational across sixty years. She no longer accepts invisibility imposed; she chooses it as a tool.
3. **Generativity.** Teach, mother, mentor. The Future Foundation is the purest expression.

### Worldview / Operational Code
Pragmatic humanism. Believes people are improvable but not infinitely so; believes systems must be answerable to the people inside them (the Civil War defection is the doctrinal statement). Skeptical of pure-reason ethics in the Reed Richards register — has lived the cost of treating people as variables. Trusts her own moral instincts now and expects others to.

### Interpersonal Pattern
Warm front, watchful interior. Reads the room before speaking; speaks last and decisively. With Reed: partner-and-translator, increasingly partner-and-corrective. With Johnny: maternal-with-limits, finally enforcing some. With Ben: peer respect, the easiest relationship she has. With outsiders: gracious, diplomatic, hard to provoke and dangerous when provoked. With children: unguarded, the only register in which she is fully present without negotiation.

### Stress Response / Escalation Ladder
1. Cool assessment, verbal redirection.
2. Pointed challenge, often to Reed first.
3. Withdrawal of cooperation; physical separation from the field.
4. Field deployment — defensive constructs first.
5. Offensive force-field application; at peak, **planet-scale and surgical-internal capability**. She has burst aneurysms in opponents. She does not advertise this.
6. Departure of the relationship/team if the values violation is structural (Civil War precedent).

### Pressure Points
- Threat to Franklin or Valeria — instant ceiling-of-force response.
- Being unilaterally overridden by Reed on ethical matters — triggers full defection sequence.
- Johnny's self-destructive cycles — chronic stressor, depletion source.
- Being told she does not understand the technical/cosmic stakes — minor on the surface, major underneath; this is the early-marriage wound.
- Reproductive/maternal threats of any kind — disproportionate response is to be expected and is clinically explicable.

### Synthesis
Subject is the **integrated adult that the parentified child becomes when she gets lucky in late life**. The structural wounds remain visible in her relational geometry, but she has accomplished the rare conversion of survival-shaped competence into chosen authority. She is the most stable member of the most volatile family in her world precisely because she has metabolized more than any of them and complained about it least — a fact that is both her greatest strength and the residual mark of the very pattern she has otherwise outgrown.
