# UATU ANALYSIS FILE: WADE WINSTON WILSON ("DEADPOOL")
## Pre-Synthesis Intelligence Workup

---

## 1. HISTORY

The subject's documented record is, by design, the least reliable in the Marvel canon. Wilson has actively corrupted his own backstory — supplying contradictory accounts to handlers, lovers, and even captive audiences — and writers across his run (Nicieza, Kelly, Simone, Way, Posehn/Duggan, Bunn, Duggan solo, Kelly again, Kibblesmith) have treated this opacity as the character's load-bearing feature rather than a continuity bug. The earliest "definitive" account (Nicieza/McGuinness, *Deadpool* vol. 3, the "Johnny Handsome"-style Weapon X arc; Kelly, *Deadpool* #-1 and #33) places early childhood in Canada with a mother who died of cancer and a father who was abusive — but Kelly's #33 explicitly stages multiple mutually exclusive origin tableaux (abusive father shot by Wade; mother as the abuser; both alive; both never existed) and lets Wilson choose. The behavior of interest is not which is true but that Wilson generates origins on demand and cannot tolerate a fixed one.

Pre-Weapon X, Wilson is established as a low-tier mercenary (special forces washout — discharged for insubordination and possible psychiatric grounds, per Nicieza's flashbacks), a contract killer in Tangier and the Balkans, romantically entangled with Vanessa Carlysle (Copycat) in a relationship marked by intensity, jokes-as-armor, and his abrupt withdrawal upon diagnosis. **Diagnosis is the formative event**: terminal cancer (sites vary by writer — brain, liver, lungs — most often metastatic and multifocal). Wilson volunteers for Department K's Weapon X offshoot (the "Workshop"/Hospice under Dr. Killebrew and Ajax/Francis), where a Wolverine-derived healing factor is bonded to him. The factor arrests the cancer by perpetually outracing it; it does not cure it. His cells regenerate so aggressively they become indistinguishable, externally, from undifferentiated tumor tissue — the disfigurement is the cure functioning, not failing.

The Hospice arc (Kelly, *Deadpool* vol. 2 / vol. 3 flashbacks) is the formative trauma proper: Wilson is repeatedly tortured by Ajax, forms a defensive trauma-bond with fellow patient Worm, kills Worm in a betting-pool incident he was manipulated into, is "killed" by Ajax, dumped, and revives. This is the **template wound** — he was promised cure, delivered torture, made to kill an attachment figure, and survived something he was meant not to survive. Every subsequent loop of behavior (betrayal anticipation, gallows performance under torture, killing-with-affection-toward-the-victim, refusal to die when convenient) reproduces this episode.

The mercenary career proper begins post-escape. Key arcs in evolutionary order:

- **Joe Kelly run (1997–1998, *Deadpool* vol. 2)**: introduction of Blind Al (captive/mother-surrogate), Weasel (only "friend"), and the messianic delusion arc where Mithras prophecy briefly offers him meaning, which he then sabotages. Establishes the **need-to-be-needed / fear-of-being-good** oscillation. The relationship with Siryn (Theresa Cassidy) introduces his pattern: pursue an idealized woman, sabotage when reciprocated.
- **Gail Simone run (*Agent X*, *Deadpool/GLI*)**: tonal stabilization, friendship with Outlaw and Sandi, evidence that competent affect regulation is possible when held by a non-threatening female social unit.
- **Daniel Way run (2008–2012)**: split-psyche literalization — the multiple internal voices (boxes) first formalized as recurring narrators. Bea Arthur fixation following grief over Blind Al's storyline; obsession with becoming an Avenger as external validation; manipulation by Norman Osborn (Dark Reign) where Wilson is comprehensively outplayed because his hunger for legitimacy makes him legible to predators.
- **Posehn/Duggan run (2012–2015)**: the most psychologically serious treatment. Marriage to Shiklah (a Succubus queen — note the object choice: a literal devourer he can't be killed by); discovery of daughter Eleanor (with Carmelita); the Butler arc revealing Wilson's family was systematically murdered to torment him; the *Original Sin* revelation that he was manipulated by Steve Rogers/SHIELD into assassinating innocents under false pretenses; followed by the Civil War II arc where, having been radicalized toward heroism, he murders Coulson under Captain America's (Hydra-Cap's) order — second catastrophic betrayal by an authority he chose to trust.
- **Duggan solo / Kelly return / Kibblesmith / Adams**: increasingly self-aware metacommentary, custody loss of Eleanor, attempts at and failures of stable domesticity, the "Mercs for Money" managerial-failure arc, the Kraken/Monster-King storyline as displacement of family-man fantasies into absurd register.

The longitudinal pattern: Wilson cycles **isolation → forced attachment (often a captive or dependent) → idealization → self-sabotage → catastrophic loss → suicidal ideation he cannot consummate → comedic dissociation → re-isolation**, with the cycle shortening over time and the metacommentary thickening as a defense.

## 2. MEDICAL

**Primary somatic facts:**

- **Metastatic carcinoma, multi-site, ongoing.** Never in remission. The healing factor (Wolverine-derived, Department K augmentation) replicates damaged cells faster than the cancer can claim them, producing a steady-state stalemate. Subject is, in oncological terms, continuously dying and continuously not-dying.
- **Disfigurement** as a chronic dermatological/oncologic consequence: skin is scar-tumor lattice over the entire body surface. Pain receptors intact; healing factor does not anesthetize. Subject experiences **continuous low-grade pain plus episodic acute pain** from regeneration of injuries that would kill a normal human.
- **Healing factor capabilities documented**: regrowth of severed limbs, decapitation survival (heads have been observed conversing while detached, *Deadpool Kills the Marvel Universe* and mainline), recovery from incineration, immunity to most toxins and pathogens, accelerated metabolism (requires extreme caloric intake; canonically eats voraciously). Telepathic resistance — the scrambled neural architecture (cancer + regeneration noise) makes mental contact aversive to telepaths (Emma Frost, Cable have both commented).
- **Neurological**: the cancer involves CNS tissue; the constant regeneration of brain cells means his neural substrate is never structurally stable. This is the canonical in-universe substrate for his cognitive disorganization — memory is unreliable not by repression alone but because the tissue encoding memory is being torn down and rebuilt. This makes the line between dissociation, delusion, and organic confabulation diagnostically irresolvable.
- **Injuries of note**: dismemberments routine; full-body skinning (Wolverine, Hulk encounters); skull replacement; multiple "deaths"; the Carbonadium-poisoning arc temporarily suppressed the healing factor.

**Substance use**: heavy alcohol use across all runs (his metabolism processes it nearly instantly, requiring volumes that are functionally self-harm rather than intoxication). Reported recreational drug use is performative — he cannot get high in any sustained way. The pursuit of intoxication he cannot achieve is itself a behavioral marker (the chase, not the result, is the point).

**Psychological impact of the soma**: (a) the body is a site of continuous betrayal — it will not let him die, it will not let him heal, it will not let him pass for human; (b) physical pain is detached from physical consequence, dissolving the normal feedback loops by which the self learns to protect itself, leading to objectively reckless self-endangerment that is **not** suicidality in the strict sense because no consummation is possible; (c) the disfigurement enforces the social isolation that his personality was already constructed around, creating a perfectly closed loop where the wound and the defense reinforce each other.

## 3. PSYCHOLOGICAL

**Attachment.** Disorganized, with strong fearful-avoidant features. Observed behaviors across stories:

- *Approach-then-sabotage with Siryn, Vanessa, Outlaw, Shiklah, Eleanor* — once attachment is reciprocated, Wilson withdraws, deceives, or stages a rupture. The trigger is reciprocity itself, suggesting an internal working model in which being seen and accepted is incompatible with continued safety (cf. Hospice: every authority figure who offered care delivered torture).
- *Captive-attachments (Blind Al, originally; Weasel via coercive friendship; the Mercs for Money managed through dependency)* — Wilson constructs relationships where he controls the exit. This is consistent with attachment under conditions where loss has been catastrophic; the captivity ensures the bond cannot be severed by the other party.
- *Idealization of unattainable maternal figures* (Bea Arthur fixation, Death personified). The libidinal object is selected for impossibility, ensuring the relationship can never enter the dangerous reciprocity phase.

**Defenses, in rough hierarchy of frequency:**

- **Humor as primary defense** — specifically, *humor that breaks frame*. Not Vaillant's mature humor (which integrates affect) but a manic-defensive variant that prevents any affect from settling. The fourth-wall breaks are diagnostically significant: they are dissociation rendered as comedy. By repeatedly asserting he is in a comic, Wilson externalizes the unbearable (his pain is not real, he is a character, none of it counts) while simultaneously confessing it (he is talking to a reader because there is no one in-frame he trusts).
- **Dissociation**, both clinical (the internal "boxes"/voices function as compartmentalized self-states — observer, id-impulse, and conscience analogs) and depersonalization-derealization (the metacommentary).
- **Reaction formation** — the murderousness of the mercenary identity sits over a striking, persistent prosocial drive (sparing innocents at cost, the Mithras arc, the Avengers obsession, repeated rescue behavior in the Posehn/Duggan and Kelly runs). The clown-killer presentation is more tolerable to him than the would-be-hero presentation, because the latter risks being judged and found wanting.
- **Identification with the aggressor** — Ajax's torture style (mockery during violence) is precisely the affective register Wilson adopts in combat.
- **Acting out** as affect regulation — when emotion threatens to consolidate (grief over Eleanor, betrayal by Rogers, loss of Vanessa) Wilson reliably escalates to violence, sex, or chaotic spectacle within the same scene.

**Affect regulation.** Poor at low intensities (cannot tolerate stillness, silence, or non-stimulated states — hence constant verbal output); paradoxically functional at extreme intensities (combat, torture, mass-casualty events), where the environment matches his internal noise floor. This is a recognizable pattern in complex PTSD: the nervous system is calibrated for crisis and dysregulates in safety.

**Cognitive style.** High verbal fluency, strong tactical pattern recognition, excellent improvisation, poor long-horizon planning, very poor in-the-moment impulse inhibition. Meta-awareness is high but **not** integrative — he can name his own pathology in real time and is not thereby changed by the naming. This is consistent with the phenomenon of insight-without-mentalization seen in some severe personality pathology, where the patient can describe the pattern with clinical precision and remain inside it.

**Self-concept.** Fundamentally split. Two stable schemas alternate: (1) *I am a monster who deserves what happened to me and who protects others by being unlovable*; (2) *I am the only one who sees the truth (the comic, the joke, the absurdity) and that makes me untouchable*. Neither permits an intermediate self-evaluation. There is no "ordinary person" position available to him internally.

**Compensatory mechanisms.**

- *Adaptive*: the humor genuinely does keep him alive (suicidal ideation is constant, consummation impossible; humor is what makes the uncloseable loop bearable). The mercenary code (refusal of certain contracts, child-protection, occasional client double-cross in favor of innocents) provides a minimal moral skeleton. The capacity for fierce, durable loyalty when the other party tolerates his testing (Cable, Bob, Weasel across long arcs) demonstrates intact attachment capacity beneath the disorganization.
- *Maladaptive*: the captive-attachment pattern; substance pursuit; the production of contradictory autobiographies that prevents anyone (including himself) from forming a coherent picture against which growth could be measured; the reflex to clown when serious mirroring is offered.

## 4. PSYCHIATRIC

Differential, derived from the above, with reasoning shown.

**Complex PTSD (ICD-11) — strongly supported.** Etiology: prolonged, inescapable interpersonal trauma (Hospice/Ajax), preceded by likely developmental adversity (the variability of his childhood accounts, combined with the affective register of all of them, indicates the *fact* of childhood adversity even where the *content* is unrecoverable). Symptom triad: (a) re-experiencing — Ajax returns as obsessional object across multiple arcs, including after Ajax's "death"; (b) avoidance via dissociation and humor; (c) hyperarousal — chronic insomnia (implied across runs), startle-equivalent escalation to violence, inability to be at rest. Plus the three CPTSD-specific clusters: affective dysregulation, negative self-concept (the monster-schema), interpersonal disturbance (the disorganized attachment).

**Major Depressive Disorder, recurrent, with chronic suicidal ideation — supported.** Persistent low-grade dysphoria; anhedonia masked by stimulation-seeking; suicidal ideation that is **chronic, ego-syntonic, and frustrated by biology** rather than absent. The relationship with Death (Thanos's rival) personifies this — his libidinal investment in his own death is unmistakable and explicitly romanticized.

**Dissociative features.** The internal voices/boxes are the central diagnostic puzzle. Differential:

- *Dissociative Identity Disorder*: partial fit. The voices are stable, distinct, and have characteristic content (one impulsive, one observational, one moralistic). They are not, however, typically full alters with separate autobiographical memory and executive control — Wilson is co-conscious with them and they function more like externalized internal-family-systems "parts." The DID label is too strong.
- *Psychotic-spectrum hallucinosis*: partial fit. The voices are auditory and ego-dystonic at times, but they are also clearly insight-preserving and structured, which is atypical for primary psychosis.
- **Best fit: trauma-related dissociation with structurally dissociated self-states**, plus a substrate of organic neurological disorganization (see Medical) producing voice-experience and confabulation on top of the dissociative architecture. The fourth-wall awareness is best read as a *depersonalization-derealization* feature elevated to a coping ideology — and it is critical that, in-universe, *he is correct*, which makes the symptom non-falsifiable and therefore non-treatable by standard reality-testing techniques.

**Personality organization.** Borderline level (Kernberg) — identity diffusion (literalized in his contradictory origins), primitive defenses (splitting, projective identification, acting out, dissociation), reality testing largely preserved but porous. Cluster B features cross multiple categories:

- *Borderline*: identity disturbance, abandonment sensitivity, affective instability, recurrent self-endangerment, transient stress-related dissociation. Strong fit.
- *Antisocial*: occupational antisocial conduct (contract killing), but lacks the core callousness — he has demonstrable empathy, guilt (often dissociated but recoverable under pressure), and is capable of altruistic action at personal cost. The mercenary career is better read as a trauma-compatible occupation than as ASPD proper.
- *Histrionic*: performative affect, attention-seeking, theatrical presentation. Secondary feature, in service of the humor defense.

**NOT supported by the record:**

- *Primary psychotic disorder (schizophrenia spectrum)* — his disorganization is trauma- and tissue-based, his voice-experience is structured, and his reality testing is preserved.
- *Bipolar I* — the manic-presenting episodes are state-dependent on stimulation rather than autonomous mood cycles.
- *ASPD as primary* — see above; the moral structure, however damaged, is intact.
- *Narcissistic Personality Disorder* — the bravado is compensatory and brittle, not entitled; he expects to be despised and is destabilized by genuine admiration rather than dependent on it.

**Etiological synthesis**: developmental adversity → military washout indicating pre-existing dysregulation → terminal diagnosis → iatrogenic torture trauma → neurological substrate damage → chronic pain + chronic dying + social rejection → stabilized pathology that the soma will not allow death to terminate. The disorder is, in a precise sense, **a CPTSD presentation that has been denied its natural endpoint** (death, dissociative collapse, or psychotic break) by a biology engineered to prevent collapse. He is held permanently at the moment of breaking.

## 5. PERSONALITY

**Core drives**, in order of operational priority:

1. *Avoid consolidation of feeling*. The single most reliable predictor of his next action is which option keeps affect moving. Combat, monologue, sex, food, the next contract — all are interchangeable so long as none of them require sitting with what just happened.
2. *Be needed without being known.* He will work tirelessly for people who depend on him and flee people who try to understand him. The captive-attachment pattern, the mercenary client structure, and the management of the Mercs for Money are all configurations that supply attachment without exposure.
3. *Test for the rejection he expects.* Every relationship is probed for its breaking point. Those that hold (Cable, Bob, Blind Al across decades, Weasel) become permanent fixtures; those that break confirm his model and are mourned without surprise.
4. *Convert pain into spectacle.* If the suffering can be made funny, it can be made survivable. This is the operational meaning of the humor — not entertainment, **metabolism**.
5. *Approach the heroic without claiming it.* The Avengers arc, the Mithras arc, the post-Original Sin redemption push, the consistent refusal of child-harm contracts — Wilson reaches toward goodness and then ensures, usually by self-sabotage, that he cannot be granted it. To be granted it would be to risk losing it.

**Worldview / operational code.** The universe is a cruel joke whose punchline is structural. Authority lies. Cures torture. Love departs or is murdered. The only stable goods are: the bit, the bond with the one or two who stayed, and the work, performed competently for cash. Within this nihilism a residual ethic survives — *do not kill children, do not betray the few, take the contract that lets you steer the outcome toward less harm rather than more*. He does not articulate this code and would deny it if named; it is observable only in his pattern of contract selection and target sparing.

**Interpersonal pattern.** Opens with deflective performance (jokes, provocation, transgression) calibrated to surface the other party's tolerance. If the other party flees or attacks, schema confirmed, relationship terminated cleanly. If the other party endures, escalates testing (cruelty disguised as comedy, unreliability, disappearance) until either rupture (expected) or stable tolerance (rare; produces lifelong loyalty). With those few who pass, his behavior is recognizably tender, often clumsy, and intensely protective. With strangers, the operational mask is total.

**Stress response and escalation.** Under acute stress: verbal output increases, fourth-wall breaks intensify, violence becomes more theatrical and less efficient (the spectacle is regulating him, not the threat). Under existential stress (loss of attachment figure, exposure of moral compromise, forced introspection): he disappears — geographically, into substances, into contract work in distant theaters — until the affect has been outrun. Under physical torture: returns to baseline humor, weaponized; this is his most regulated state because it is the state his nervous system was trained for.

**Pressure points.**

- *Children at risk* — overrides all other priorities, including self-preservation and contract obligation. Likely linked to the unrecoverable childhood and to Eleanor.
- *Being seen with sincerity* — destabilizes him more than any threat. Mirroring without judgment will reliably trigger flight, deflection, or escalation.
- *Authority figures offering legitimacy* — he is dangerously legible to anyone who frames an ask as "we believe in you, you can be one of us" (Osborn, Hydra-Rogers). The hunger to be admitted to the moral community is his largest exploitable surface.
- *Ajax, and Ajax-coded figures* — sadistic clinicians, torturers who mock, anyone who couples cruelty with care-language. Provokes regression and obsession.
- *His own face, unmediated* — mirrors, photographs, recognition by strangers. He has organized his life to avoid this stimulus and reacts to forced exposure with disproportionate violence or collapse-into-humor.

**Prognostic note for synthesis stage.** The subject is not, in the ordinary sense, treatable, because the biological substrate prevents the collapse-and-reconstitution that growth requires for a personality this disorganized. What stabilizes him is not insight (which he has in abundance and cannot use) but the slow accumulation of attachments that have survived his testing. The trajectory across six decades of record is, very slightly and very unevenly, toward a wider circle of such attachments and a marginally longer interval between cycles of self-sabotage. He is improving at approximately the rate his biology permits, which is: barely, and only because he cannot die of it.
