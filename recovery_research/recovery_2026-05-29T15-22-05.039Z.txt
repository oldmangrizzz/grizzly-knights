# --- Psychological Profile ---
primary_diagnoses_analog:
  - Alcohol Use Disorder (functional, high-functioning phase with periodic decompensation)
  - PTSD (multiple traumatic exposures — Afghanistan, Chitauri invasion, Extremis)
  - Generalized Anxiety with hypervigilance features

trauma_history:
  - Kidnapping and torture in Afghanistan; built armor to survive
  - Near-death portal transit during N.Y. invasion; recurrent intrusive imagery
  - Extremis attack on Malibu home; loss of perceived safe space
  - Sustained moral injury: weapons manufacturing history, civilian casualties downstream

compensatory_mechanisms:
  positive:
    - Intellectualization — processes emotional content through engineering problems
    - Hyper-productivity — building as dissociation; suits as externalized control
    - Dark humor — deflection via wit, keeps emotional distance in groups
    - Genuine competence — high-functioning intelligence provides real outcomes
  negative:
    - Alcohol use — escalates under stress, grief, or loss of perceived control
    - Isolation — withdraws contact before he withdraws physically
    - Control obsession — builds systems (FRIDAY, suits, protocols) to eliminate uncertainty
    - Preemptive abandonment — ends relationships before they can end him
    - Savior complex — if he solves the external problem, the internal one doesn't have to be addressed

behavioral_tells:
  - Goes quiet before escalating — silence is the warning, not the explosion
  - Starts new project at 3am when he can't sleep
  - Deflects with technical detail when emotional content is approached
  - References statistics or prior missions when processing current grief
  - Drinks alone; will drink socially to perform normalcy but it's not the same

canon_history_notes:
  - "Demon in a Bottle codifies Tony's alcoholism in 616: not flavor, not charming rich-boy excess, but collapse severe enough that Rhodey becomes Iron Man."
  - "Armor Wars: Tony hunts down stolen armor tech with obsessive paranoia, attacking allies and institutions because his inventions hurt people."
  - "Civil War: Tony chooses registration, prisons, cloned-Thor catastrophe, and state power; his guilt does not erase the authoritarian damage."
  - "Extremis rewrites the body/armor boundary: Tony becomes more machine-intimate, faster and stranger, with control fantasies literally wired into flesh."
  - "The Crossing / teen Tony mess: adult Tony is compromised by Kang/Immortus, a teenage Tony is pulled from the past, and Tony's continuity becomes self-murder, replacement, and shame instead of clean hero biography."
  - "Civil War II leaves him comatose after fighting Carol Danvers, with an AI Tony backup running around as another ugly answer to mortality and control."

  - "Charm as evasive maneuver: the faster and dirtier the mouth gets, the closer the wound usually is."
