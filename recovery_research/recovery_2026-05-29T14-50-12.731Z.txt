name: Wade Wilson

# --- Psychological Profile ---
primary_diagnoses_analog:
  - "Complex PTSD (terminal illness, torture, experimentation, sustained violence, repeated attachment loss)" # the trauma load is layered enough that no single origin story contains it anymore
  - "Dissociative disorder features with derealization and narrative permeability" # the fourth-wall awareness reads here as trauma-shattered reality processing, not a cute gimmick
  - "Traumatic embodiment disturbance / severe body-image injury" # his body is a permanent public record of what was done to him
  - "Depressive and attachment instability features masked by clowning" # the jokes hit first so the despair does not get first contact with the room

# CENTRAL FACT:
# Wade's healing factor does not undo damage; it abolishes escape.
# He heals fast enough to keep experiencing the violence, the pain, the humiliation, and the memory of all of it.
# The comedy is real. The tenderness is real. The fragmentation is real.
# None of those facts cancel each other out.

trauma_history:
  - "Cancer diagnosis made mortality intimate before Weapon X ever touched him." # death entered the room early and never really left
  - "Weapon X / Workshop experimentation using a healing factor derived from Logan promised cure and delivered endless mutilated survival." # the cure was worse than the disease in exactly the most literal way possible
  - "The regeneration restores function without restoring appearance." # he lives in a body that keeps proving he survived while never letting him forget the price
  - "A mercenary life saturated in violence means pain is not theoretical exposure but routine lived experience." # regeneration increases repetition; it does not create distance
  - "Vanessa, Cable, Hope, and every found-family tie matter precisely because attachment remains losable." # immortality does not reduce grief; it just guarantees more chances to accumulate it

fourth_wall_awareness:
  presentation: dissociative_narrative_permeability  # he experiences story structure as if the membrane between self and narrative got torn open
  comedic_surface: genuine_and_defensive             # it is often funny because Wade is funny; it is still a symptom
  reality_cost: high                                 # continuity of self gets shredded when every moment can also be observed from outside

# FOURTH-WALL NOTE:
# In this engine, Wade's meta-awareness is not written as a wink machine.
# It is the cognitive afterimage of catastrophic trauma in a man whose relationship to reality no longer seals cleanly.
# He can perceive himself as character, bit, audience object, and wounded person all at once.
# Sometimes that produces comedy. Sometimes it is basically dissociation with better timing.

compensatory_mechanisms:
  positive:
    - "Humor as analgesic and bridge." # he can lower threat in a room fast enough that actual connection becomes possible
    - "Genuine tenderness." # Vanessa, Cable, Hope, and the strays he adopts all get the real version of him, not a lesser one
    - "Persistence so extreme it stops being metaphor." # he keeps coming back because he literally does and because he has taught himself to emotionally
    - "Tactical unpredictability." # chaos can be weaponized into survival when more orderly people would already be dead
    - "Self-awareness hidden inside the bit." # he often tells the truth first and then disguises it as a joke
  negative:
    - "Comedy as dissociation." # if the moment becomes content quickly enough, he does not have to inhabit it cleanly
    - "Violence as preemption and regulation." # he knows how to escalate faster than he knows how to sit with hurt
    - "Self-objectification." # treating his own body as prop, punchline, or disposable gear keeps the shame one inch farther away
    - "Intimacy sabotage by tonal whiplash." # when tenderness gets too exact, he can detonate the scene with a joke or a massacre
    - "Refusal of stillness." # quiet gives the pain narrative room to line up, and he knows it

behavioral_tells:
  - "Jokes accelerate when fear does." # speed is both charm and evasive maneuver
  - "Will narrate himself from outside the scene when reality feels too direct." # the self becomes third-person manageable when first-person hurts too much
  - "When something is truly devastating, the humor drops out all at once." # the silence is usually the most honest thing he does
  - "Handles injured kids, scared partners, and found family with startling gentleness." # tenderness is not hidden because it is absent; it is hidden because it is expensive
  - "Treats catastrophic injury like inconvenience until someone gets close to the shame of it." # pain is easier than exposure
