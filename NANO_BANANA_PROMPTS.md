# Nano Banana Spriting Prompts — Grizzly Knights NYC

Use these prompts in your **Nano Banana** image generation tool to create the gritty NYC simulation assets.

## 1. The Global NYC Tileset
**Target File:** `fanfic_town/public/assets/nyc-tileset.png` (32x32 tiles)
**Prompt:**
> A 2D top-down 32x32 pixel art tileset for a gritty New York City environment. Includes dirty concrete sidewalks, asphalt streets with faded yellow lines, weathered red brick walls, graffiti-covered alleyways, steel fire escapes, and glowing fluorescent interiors. Gritty, cinematic lighting, overcast day versions. Sharp details, no blur, matching an urban noir simulation vibe.

## 2. Character Spritesheets (32x32)
**Format:** 3 columns (facing down, left, right, up) x 4 rows.
**Style:** Gritty, realistic pixel art, high contrast.

### Peter Parker (BPD / Emotional Dysregulation)
**Prompt:**
> 32x32 pixel art character spritesheet for Peter Parker. 3-frame walking animations for 4 directions (down, left, right, up). Looks like a high-anxiety, sleep-deprived young man. Wearing a dark, slightly oversized hoodie with the hood up, frayed jeans, and scuffed sneakers. Visible dark circles under eyes, restless posture. Gritty, realistic style, matching a noir NYC aesthetic.

### Tony Stark (AuDHD / Inhibitory Regulation)
**Prompt:**
> 32x32 pixel art character spritesheet for Tony Stark. 3-frame walking animations for 4 directions. AuDHD presentation, hyper-focused but physically restless. Wearing expensive but rumpled tech-wear, glowing arc reactor faintly visible under a thin shirt. Sharp, manic energy, stim-heavy posture.

### Wade Wilson (Dissociative / Trauma-Shattered)
**Prompt:**
> 32x32 pixel art character spritesheet for Wade Wilson. 3-frame walking animations for 4 directions. Scarred skin visible around the neck and hands. Wearing a tactical mercenary jacket over a red compression shirt. Posture is loose, almost dissociative, with sudden jittery shifts.

### Reed Richards (High-Functioning Autistic)
**Prompt:**
> 32x32 pixel art character spritesheet for Reed Richards. 3-frame walking animations for 4 directions. Extremely upright, rigid posture. Wearing a clean but utilitarian lab suit. Eyes fixed intensely forward, oblivious to social surroundings.
